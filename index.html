How Salary Is Affected By Socio-Demographic Variables

Introduction

Over the past decade, especially in recent years, we have seen a wide variety of factors that have affected a person's salary. Does your degree dictate how much you will make? Or perhaps your experience? Or maybe it's some other factor?

In this project, I will be using a dataset that focuses on salary and demographic information with additional details on years of experience. This dataset will help us find the relationship of income and various socio-demographic variables. During this project, we will be using LiveSQL, Excel, and Tableau to help us uncover patterns and correlations and shed light on income disparities and socio-economic dynamics.
